What is Generous Baby Lions ?



Generous Baby Lions is a collection of 6666 hand-drawn and randomlygenerated NFTs living on the POLYGON blockchain.

Each GBL is unique and has its own personality.

Holding one will give you access to :

-The giveaways (400k $ in total),

-The community wallet whose initial stake will be 50k and where 100%
of the royalties will go.

In addition, if you are a VIP or OG and hold a GBL, you will have more chances to win the giveaways and you will also have the honour of being able to participate in the decision making process for the giveaways (For the dollar prizes, you will be able to choose the amount of money to be won as well as the number of people who will win) and you will have a place of honour for the future projects!

The GBLs fight everyday to improve the NFT world. But now they need help form you guys !

OUR STORY

In a merciless virtual world where thousands of scams are running, a group of unusual looking individuals decided to change things. They wanted to be known as the Generous Baby Lions.

Their goal is simple, to build a community that trusts them.This community will be there to help the Generous Baby Lions achieve their ultimate goal: giving their profits from their project back to the people who made it happen: the community.

The Generous Baby lions is a project whose sole purpose is to giveaway money and gifts back to the community. That is why we decided to giveaway most of our profits ($400,000) to you guys! Our goal is to lift each other up and we hope this project will help improve the NFT world.

FAQ


  • When will the mint take place ?

    All the infomations will be communicated on our discord in a specially dedicated channel.If you see any information about the date of the mint circulating outside our discord or our official website, please consider it as false and report it to us on discord.












  • Why polygon ?

    On the polygon blockchain, transaction fees are very low compared to many other blockchain networks such as Ethereum for example. We want people to pay the real price for our project and not gigantic fees like on the Ethereum network for example.
    If you do not know how to install metamask or how to get the polygon network on metamask, just watch this video. ;)








  • When will the giveaways take place ?

    Apart from the pre-sale, the giveaways will take place post mint. The reason is simple, we want to reward the holders for trusting our project.








  • Who will receive the rewards promised in the road map ?

    All rewards will be distributed to holders, EXCEPT for the pre-sale giveaway where we will choose randomly 10 members of our Discord who will have the chance to win 1k each.







  • Can I choose to receive the reward in the crypto of my choice ?

    Regarding giveaways involving ONLY DOLLAR PRIZES, note that winners are free to choose the crypto of their choice.








  • Why give back to the community ?

    First of all to thank those who believe in our project. We (the team) have had some very bad experiences with the NFT world... In all honesty it is quite easy to create a solid project that benefits everyone. That's why, instead of watching and being passive, we decided to create this project to lift each other and to help improve the NFT world.
